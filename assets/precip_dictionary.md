## Precipitation

### Data Dictionary

|variable       |class     |description |
|:--------------|:---------|:-----------|
| `lon`         |numeric   |Longitude   |
| `lat`         |numeric   |Latitude    |
| `year`        |numeric   |Year        |
| `mm`          |numeric   |Month       |
| `dd`          |numeric   |Day of month|
| `doy`         |numeric   |Day of year |
| `prectotcorr` |numeric   |The bias corrected average of total precipitation at the surface of the earth in water mass (includes water content in snow).|
| `qv2m`        |numeric   |The ratio of the mass of water vapor to the total mass of air at 2 meters (g water/kg total air).|
| `rh2m`        |numeric   |The ratio of actual partial pressure of water vapor to the partial pressure at saturation, expressed in percent.|
| `cloud_amt`   |numeric   |The average percent of cloud amount during the temporal period.|
| `cloud_od`    |numeric   |The vertical optical thickness between the top and bottom of a cloud.|
| `pw`          |numeric   |The total atmospheric water vapor contained in a vertical column of the atmosphere.|
